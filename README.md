## Sales performance vs Budget variance analysis

---
## Project Overview
It is a sales performance analysis versus budget which identifies the main cause of underperformance and indicates where the actionable knowledge lies in each region, each product, and each customer segment.  
The dashboard is aimed at the executive level decision making that highly emphasizes on the variance analysis and profitability.

---

## Business Problem
The company had a deficit on the annual sales budget. Through this analysis, the aim was to:
.Determine where the sales were below budgeted.
.Know what products and customer segments delivered.
.Offer recommendations to the management based on data.

---

## Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Sales transactions
- Budget data
- Product, customer, and time dimensions




----


## Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin (%)
- Sales Variance vs Budget
- Sales Variance %


## Key Insights

#Executive Summary
- Sales missed the annual budget by **Â£0.4M**
- All regions underperformed, with **Clothing and Accessories** driving most of the shortfall
- Sales performance improved in the second half of the year, indicating recovery momentum

## Product Performance
- **Bikes** category generated the majority of revenue and profit with strong margins
- Accessories and Clothing contributed minimal profit despite sales presence
- High profit concentration presents a portfolio risk

## Customer Insights
- **Middle and upper-middle income customers** generated the highest sales and profit
- Families with children were the strongest revenue-driving segment
- Low-income segments contributed less value despite customer volume

---

## Business Recommendations
- Focus investment and promotions on high-margin bike products
- Review pricing and cost structures for low-margin categories
- Target marketing efforts toward middle and upper-middle income families
